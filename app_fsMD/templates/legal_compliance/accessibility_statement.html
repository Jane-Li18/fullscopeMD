ACCESSIBILITY STATEMENT

Last Updated: December 5, 2025

Our goal

We’re committed to making FullScopeMD accessible and usable for everyone, including people with disabilities.

Contact Support

FullScopeMD is committed to ensuring that our website, digital services, and telehealth platform are accessible and usable for all individuals, including people with disabilities. We strive to comply with applicable accessibility standards and to provide an inclusive and user-friendly experience for every patient.

1. Our Commitment

We aim to follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards, which outline requirements for improving accessibility for people with visual, auditory, cognitive, and motor disabilities.

2. Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. FullScopeMD regularly reviews and updates our digital properties to improve:

  • Navigation and readability
  • Color contrast and font clarity
  • Compatibility with screen readers and assistive technologies
  • Keyboard and voice-navigation support
  • Mobile and tablet accessibility

We may also work with third-party vendors to help ensure accessibility compliance across all web components.

3. Third-Party Content

Some parts of our website or platform may include content from third parties (such as payment processors, telehealth systems, or pharmacy portals). While we cannot control third-party accessibility standards, we encourage them to provide accessible solutions and continually evaluate alternatives if accessibility barriers are identified.

4. If You Need Assistance

If you experience difficulty accessing any part of our website or services, or if you need information in a different format, please contact us:

FullScopeMD – Accessibility Support
Email: support@fullscopemd.com
Phone: [XXX-XXX-XXXX]
Please include:
  • The page, feature, or content you were trying to access
  • The type of issue you encountered
  • Any assistive technology you were using (if applicable)

We will do our best to respond promptly and provide reasonable accommodations.

5. Feedback Welcome

We value your feedback. If you have suggestions for improving accessibility or usability, please let us know at any time. By using the FullScopeMD website and digital services, you acknowledge that accessibility improvements are ongoing and that we are committed to providing the best possible experience for all users.

Refund Policy

REFUND POLICY

Last Updated: December 5, 2025

Please read carefully

This Refund Policy explains when charges may or may not be refunded for FullScopeMD services and purchases.

Contact Support

1. Overview

FullScopeMD provides telehealth consultations, treatment programs, and prescription services. We strive to deliver high-quality care and clear expectations regarding billing and refunds. This Refund Policy explains when charges may or may not be refunded. By using the Services or completing a purchase, you agree to this Refund Policy.

2. Telehealth Consultations (Non-Refundable Once Rendered)

All telehealth consultations, evaluations, chart reviews, and medical services are NON-REFUNDABLE once a licensed clinician has:

  • Reviewed your clinical intake or medical history, OR
  • Initiated or completed a consultation, OR
  • Provided medical advice, assessment, or treatment determination.
Important
Once clinical services are rendered, refunds cannot be issued under any circumstance.

3. Medication Orders — Eligibility for Cancellation

A medication order may be canceled and refunded ONLY IF ALL of the following are true:

  • A prescription has NOT yet been issued by the clinician, AND
  • The pharmacy has NOT begun fulfilling, compounding, preparing, or packaging the medication, AND
  • A tracking number has NOT been generated, AND
  • The order has NOT been handed to the shipping courier.

If these conditions are met, the order may be eligible for cancellation and refund upon request.

4. Medication Orders — Non-Refundable Once Fulfilled or Shipped

Medication orders become FINAL and NON-REFUNDABLE once ANY of the following occur:

  • A clinician issues a prescription, OR
  • The pharmacy begins preparation, mixing, or compounding, OR
  • A tracking number is generated, OR
  • The package is handed to the courier for shipment.

Federal and state pharmacy regulations prohibit returning, reselling, or reusing prescription medications once prepared or dispensed.

5. Delivered Medications (Final Sale)

All medications are FINAL SALE once delivered to the patient’s provided address.

No refunds, returns, replacements, or exchanges will be issued for:

  • Delivered medications
  • Unopened or sealed medications
  • Medications refused, abandoned, or returned by the carrier

This policy exists to comply with safety, handling, and pharmacy regulations.

6. No Refunds for Lack of Results or Treatment Response

FullScopeMD does NOT issue refunds based on:

  • Dissatisfaction with results
  • Perceived lack of effectiveness
  • Variation in expected outcomes
  • Slower progress than anticipated

Individual response to medication varies significantly based on:

  • Body chemistry and metabolism
  • Dosage adherence
  • Diet, lifestyle, and exercise
  • Medical conditions and hormone levels
  • Genetics and absorption differences

Because outcomes cannot be guaranteed and compounded medications are batch-prepared according to strict standards—no refunds are provided for treatment results.

7. Shipping Issues (Lost, Damaged, or Delayed Packages)

If a package is lost, damaged, or delayed, we will work with the pharmacy and shipping carrier to determine the appropriate resolution. Depending on circumstances, a replacement may be issued.

Refunds cannot be issued for medications already prepared, shipped, or delivered.

FullScopeMD is not responsible for delivery issues caused by incorrect shipping information provided by the patient.

8. Billing Errors or Duplicate Charges

If you believe you were incorrectly charged:

  • Contact us at support@fullscopemd.com.
  • Verified duplicate or erroneous charges will be refunded promptly.

9. Promotions, Discounts, and Coupons

Promotional codes and discounts:

  • Cannot be applied retroactively
  • Cannot be exchanged for cash value
  • Do not guarantee clinical eligibility
  • Do not override refund restrictions

10. Acceptance of This Policy

By receiving telehealth services or completing a purchase through FullScopeMD, you acknowledge and agree to this Refund Policy. This Policy forms part of the FullScopeMD Terms & Conditions and applies to all transactions.

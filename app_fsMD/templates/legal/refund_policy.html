1. Overview

FullScopeMD provides telehealth consultations, treatment programs, and prescription services through licensed U.S. healthcare providers. We are committed to transparency regarding billing, refunds, and patient expectations.

This Refund Policy explains when charges may or may not be refunded. By using FullScopeMD services or completing a purchase, you agree to this Refund Policy, which forms part of our Terms & Conditions.

2. Payment Collection & Clinical Review

Payment is collected at checkout to authorize services and initiate the clinical review process. Our payment processor does not support holding or escrowing funds prior to consultation.

Payment submission does not guarantee clinical approval or prescription issuance. All treatment decisions are made solely by licensed medical providers based on clinical appropriateness and patient safety.

3. Clinical Denial (Full Refund Eligibility)

If a licensed provider determines that a patient is not clinically eligible for treatment before a prescription is issued and before any medication preparation or fulfillment begins:

  • The order will be canceled
  • A full refund will be issued to the original payment method

FullScopeMD does not charge separate fees for consultations or shipping. Patients who are clinically denied prior to prescription issuance are eligible for a full refund.

4. Telehealth Services (Non-Refundable Once Rendered and Approved)

Once a licensed provider has reviewed the patient’s intake, approved treatment, and issued a prescription, the associated telehealth services are considered rendered and are non-refundable.

This includes:

  • Clinical intake review
  • Medical evaluation
  • Treatment determination
  • Prescription issuance
Important
Refunds cannot be issued once treatment has been approved and clinical services have been completed.

5. Medication Orders Eligibility for Cancellation

A medication order may be canceled and refunded only if all of the following conditions are met:

  • A prescription has not been issued by the clinician
  • The pharmacy has not begun fulfilling, compounding, preparing, or packaging the medication
  • A tracking number has not been generated
  • The order has not been handed to the shipping courier

6. Medication Orders Non-Refundable Once Fulfilled or Shipped

Medication orders become final and non-refundable once any of the following occur:

  • A clinician issues a prescription
  • The pharmacy begins preparation, mixing, or compounding
  • A tracking number is generated
  • The package is handed to the courier for shipment

Federal and state pharmacy regulations prohibit returning, reselling, or reusing prescription medications once prepared or dispensed.

7. Delivered Medications (Final Sale)

All medications are final sale once delivered to the patient’s provided address.

No refunds, returns, replacements, or exchanges will be issued for:

  • Delivered medications
  • Unopened or sealed medications
  • Medications refused, abandoned, or returned by the carrier

This policy exists to comply with medication safety, handling, and pharmacy regulations.

8. No Refunds for Lack of Results or Treatment Response

FullScopeMD does not issue refunds based on:

  • Dissatisfaction with results
  • Perceived lack of effectiveness
  • Variation in expected outcomes
  • Slower progress than anticipated

Individual response to medication varies based on factors including:

  • Body chemistry and metabolism
  • Dosage adherence
  • Diet, lifestyle, and exercise
  • Medical conditions and hormone levels
  • Genetics and absorption differences

Because outcomes cannot be guaranteed, refunds are not provided based on treatment results.

9. Shipping Issues (Lost, Damaged, or Delayed Packages)

If a package is lost, damaged, or delayed, FullScopeMD will work with the pharmacy and shipping carrier to determine the appropriate resolution. In some cases, a replacement may be issued.

  • Refunds cannot be issued for medications that have already been prepared, shipped, or delivered.
  • FullScopeMD is not responsible for delivery issues caused by incorrect or incomplete shipping information provided by the patient.

10. Billing Errors or Duplicate Charges

If you believe you were incorrectly charged:

  • Please contact us at support@fullscopemd.com.
  • Verified duplicate or erroneous charges will be refunded promptly.

11. Promotions, Discounts, and Coupons

Promotional codes and discounts:

  • Cannot be applied retroactively
  • Cannot be exchanged for cash value
  • Do not guarantee clinical eligibility
  • Do not override refund restrictions outlined in this policy

12. Acceptance of This Policy

By receiving telehealth services or completing a purchase through FullScopeMD, you acknowledge and agree to this Refund Policy. This Policy applies to all transactions and forms part of the FullScopeMD Terms & Conditions.

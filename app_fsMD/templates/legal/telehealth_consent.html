1. Purpose of This Consent

This Telehealth Informed Consent explains how telehealth works, outlines its benefits and risks, and documents your agreement to receive healthcare services through telehealth provided by or through FullScopeMD (“FullScopeMD,” “we,” “us,” or “our”). By proceeding with telehealth services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the information below.

2. What Is Telehealth?

“Telehealth” (or “telemedicine”) includes the delivery of healthcare services using electronic communications and technologies instead of in-person visits. Telehealth may involve:

  • Live audio and/or video communication
  • Secure messaging or chat
  • Reviewing information you submit electronically (asynchronous telehealth)
  • Uploading medical records, photos, forms, or lab results
  • Remote monitoring or digital tools, where applicable

Telehealth services may include evaluation, diagnosis, treatment recommendations, prescriptions when appropriate, follow-up care, and general medical guidance.

3. Providers and Licensing

Telehealth services are provided by independently licensed healthcare professionals (physicians, nurse practitioners, physician assistants, or other qualified clinicians). You understand that:

  • Clinicians must be licensed in the U.S. state where you are physically located during the telehealth encounter.
  • You must accurately report your physical location at the time of the visit.
  • You may be referred for in-person evaluation if clinically necessary.

4. Potential Benefits of Telehealth

Potential benefits may include:

  • More convenient access to licensed clinicians
  • Reduced travel and waiting time
  • Faster follow-up and ongoing communication
  • Ability to share records electronically

5. Potential Risks and Limitations of Telehealth

Telehealth also has potential risks, including:

  • Technical failures (poor connection, audio/video issues, software malfunction)
  • Possible delays or incomplete information if your device or connection is limited
  • Rare but possible unauthorized access to electronic data
  • Telehealth may not be appropriate for all medical conditions
  • A clinician may determine that in-person care is necessary

No specific result, diagnosis, or treatment outcome can be guaranteed.

6. Telehealth Is Not for Emergencies

Emergency Notice
Telehealth is not appropriate for medical emergencies. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, or believe your condition may be life-threatening, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room immediately.

7. Privacy, Security, and Confidentiality

FullScopeMD uses industry-standard safeguards to protect your information. Telehealth may involve the electronic transmission of medical information, which may include Protected Health Information (“PHI”) governed by HIPAA. You understand that:

  • Your health information will be protected and used in accordance with our Privacy Policy and our HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices.
  • Electronic communication systems may have security vulnerabilities despite reasonable safeguards.
  • You should use a private location and secure internet connection when participating in telehealth services.

FullScopeMD does not record video or audio telehealth sessions without your consent, unless required by law. You agree not to record sessions unless you have been granted written permission from FullScopeMD or your clinician.

8. Use and Disclosure of Information

Your information may be used or disclosed for:

  • Treatment (care, diagnosis, prescriptions, coordination with pharmacies or labs)
  • Payment (billing, processing transactions, financial records)
  • Healthcare operations (quality review, training, customer support, compliance)
  • Other purposes permitted by law and described in the Privacy Policy and HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices

FullScopeMD may share information with pharmacies, laboratories, clinicians, and technology vendors who support the Services. Vendors handling PHI are required to comply with privacy and security obligations.

9. Your Responsibilities

As a telehealth patient, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate and complete information about your health
  • Share your true physical location during the telehealth visit
  • Participate from a private, safe environment
  • Use devices and internet connections you believe to be secure
  • Follow the treatment instructions provided or discuss concerns with your clinician
  • Inform your clinician of any changes to your condition

Failing to provide accurate information may affect the quality of your care.

10. No Guarantee of Results

You understand and agree that:

  • Telehealth does not guarantee specific outcomes or improvements.
  • Medication response varies significantly between individuals.
  • Lifestyle, adherence, medical history, and biological factors influence results.
  • FullScopeMD makes no promises regarding weight loss, wellness outcomes, or medical improvement.

11. Financial Responsibility

You acknowledge that:

  • You are responsible for all fees associated with telehealth services and prescribed medications, in accordance with the FullScopeMD Terms & Conditions and Refund Policy.
  • Some services may not be covered by insurance or FullScopeMD may not submit claims to insurance.
  • You are responsible for confirming insurance coverage, if applicable.

12. Right to Withdraw Consent

You have the right to withdraw consent for telehealth at any time. Withdrawal will not affect care already provided; however, it may limit your ability to continue receiving services from FullScopeMD if telehealth is required for your program.

13. Acknowledgment and Agreement

By scheduling, initiating, or participating in a telehealth visit, you confirm that:

  • You have read and understand this Telehealth Informed Consent.
  • You have had an opportunity to ask questions and have them answered.
  • You consent to receive healthcare services via telehealth for yourself (and/or for your minor or dependent, if applicable).
  • You understand that you may withdraw consent at any time by notifying us, which may impact your ability to receive certain services.
  • If you do not agree to this Telehealth Informed Consent, you should not use our telehealth services.

By clicking “I Agree,” checking the consent box, scheduling a visit, or participating in a telehealth encounter, you provide your valid and binding electronic consent to receive telehealth services from FullScopeMD.

1. Introduction

FullScopeMD (“FullScopeMD,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our websites, applications, telehealth platforms, and related services (“Services”). By accessing or using the Services, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, you must discontinue use of the Services.

2. Relationship to Our HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices

When FullScopeMD provides clinical services, certain information qualifies as Protected Health Information (“PHI”) under HIPAA. Our HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices (“HIPAA NPP”) explains how PHI may be used or disclosed for treatment, payment, and healthcare operations. The HIPAA NPP governs PHI in the event of any conflict with this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy applies broadly to all personal information collected through the Services, including information that is not classified as PHI.

  • Name
  • Date of birth
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Mailing address
  • Account login credentials
  • Identity verification information
  • Payment information (handled by secure third-party processors)
B. Protected Health Information (PHI)
  • Medical history and health conditions
  • Symptoms, diagnoses, and treatment plans
  • Medication history and prescriptions
  • Lab orders and results
  • Images, documents, or forms you upload

C. Technical and Device Information
  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device identifiers
  • Operating system
  • Pages or screens viewed
  • Time and duration of visits
  • App usage metrics
D. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies, web beacons, and analytics tools to:

  • Maintain session security
  • Improve performance
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Personalize user experience

You may manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

E. Information from Third Parties
  • Pharmacies
  • Labs
  • Healthcare providers
  • Payment processors
  • Referral or marketing partners
  • Identity verification services

A. For Treatment and Clinical Services
  • Conducting telehealth visits
  • Reviewing clinical history
  • Coordinating care with clinicians, pharmacies, or labs
  • Issuing prescriptions (when appropriate)
B. For Payment and Billing
  • Processing payments
  • Managing subscriptions or auto-renewals
  • Maintaining financial and accounting records
C. For Healthcare Operations
  • Quality review and improvement
  • Customer support
  • Platform maintenance
  • System security and fraud prevention
  • Compliance, auditing, and reporting
D. For Service-Related Communications

  • Prescription updates

  • Account security alerts

  • Product or service announcements

You may opt out at any time via the unsubscribe link or by replying STOP to SMS.

F. For Legal, Security, or Regulatory Purposes
  • Complying with laws and regulations
  • Responding to subpoenas or court orders
  • Investigating security incidents or fraud
  • Protecting the rights, safety, and property of users and FullScopeMD

5. How We Share Your Information

A. With Healthcare Providers
We share PHI with independent licensed clinicians who provide your care.
B. With Pharmacies and Labs
We share necessary information to:
  • Transmit prescriptions
  • Coordinate medication dispensing
  • Process lab orders and receive results
C. With Service Providers and Business Associates
We may share information with trusted third parties who help us operate the Services, including telehealth platforms, data hosting and cloud services, SMS and email communication vendors, customer support platforms, identity verification tools, and analytics providers. Where PHI is involved, these entities sign a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) as required by HIPAA.
D. With Legal or Regulatory Authorities
We may disclose information when required by law or to protect health and safety.
E. With Your Authorization
We may share information with third parties when you provide written consent. We do not sell your PHI or personal information.

6. Cookies, Analytics, and Tracking Technologies

We may use analytics tools to understand usage and enhance performance. These tools may collect usage data, but we do not permit analytics vendors to access PHI unless they are under a BAA. You may opt out of certain analytics features by adjusting browser or device settings.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access your information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of certain information
  • Request restrictions on certain uses or disclosures
  • Obtain a copy of your medical records (HIPAA)
  • Request an accounting of PHI disclosures (HIPAA)
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise your rights, contact us using the information below.

8. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Access controls and authentication measures
  • Secure servers and firewalls
  • Regular risk assessments and monitoring
  • HIPAA-compliant data storage solutions

While no system is completely secure, we take reasonable measures to protect personal information and PHI.

9. Data Retention

We retain information as required to provide services, maintain medical records under state and federal law, meet legal, regulatory, and reporting obligations, resolve disputes, and support business operations. Medical records are retained according to state-specific retention laws.

10. Children’s Privacy

FullScopeMD does not knowingly collect information from children under 18 without parental or guardian authorization. Services for minors require consent and account oversight from a parent or legal guardian.

11. Third-Party Links

Our Services may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party sites. We encourage reviewing their privacy policies before providing personal information.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When updates occur, the “Last Updated” date will be revised. Continued use of the Services after changes means you accept the updated Policy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find clear answers about your care.

Get quick answers to the most common questions about becoming a patient, your providers, pricing, and what happens if you experience side effects.

Eligibility depends on your state of residence, your health history, and the type of care you’re looking for.

Once you complete your intake form, a U.S. licensed provider will personally review your details to see if our GLP-1 weight loss program, peptide therapy, skin treatments, hair-loss care, or primary care services are a good and safe fit for you.

Your care is handled by U.S. licensed clinicians, including physicians, nurse practitioners, and physician assistants who are fully authorized to treat patients in your state.

Each treatment plan, prescription, and dose adjustment is reviewed by a licensed provider to make sure you’re receiving safe, attentive, high-quality care every step of the way.

FullScopeMD operates on a straightforward cash-pay model so you always know exactly what you’re paying for.

Your visit, follow-up messaging, and treatment plan are included in one clear price. Medication costs may vary by program, and many patients choose to use their FSA or HSA cards if eligible.

Your care doesn’t end after your first appointment. If you have concerns, side effects, or feel your progress has slowed, reach out to our team.

We can arrange a follow-up visit, adjust your dosage, or explore alternative treatments as needed. If an in-person evaluation is ever recommended, we’ll guide you on what to do next.
